Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NITOR

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment using the appointment form on this website, or call us directly at +88-02-55058902

What are your emergency services available?

We provide 24/7 emergency services for trauma, orthopedic injuries, and related conditions with experienced surgeons on-call always.

Do you accept all insurance plans?

We accept most insurance plans. Please contact the billing department at +88-02-55058903 for specific plan details.

What is your visiting hours?

OPD visiting hours: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily. Emergency services: 24/7. In-patient visiting: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM.

Can I get my reports online?

Yes, you can collect your medical reports and test results from the hospital. Contact our record department for details.
